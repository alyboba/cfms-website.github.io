Wellness

These school-specific wellness resource sheets are intended primarily as summaries of important wellness information for students attending the selected university; however, information included in the resources may also be useful to visiting students. Please note that not all resources listed in these resource sheets will be available to visiting students. The work of the CFMS Wellness Committee members in creating their school's resource sheet is acknowledged and very much appreciated.
