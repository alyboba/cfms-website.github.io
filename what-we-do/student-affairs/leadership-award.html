MD Leadership Awards

The CFMS and MD Financial Management want to recognize some of the passionate, dedicated and caring medical student leaders across Canada who have made innovative contributions to their schools and communities. In recognition of their achievements, one student from each of the 14 member schools of the Canadian Federation of Medical Students (CFMS) will receive a Medical Student Leadership Award.

To apply, please login at the old CFMS website by clicking here.

We apologize for the inconvenience as we complete the process of migrating to the new site.
