Press Releases

Media Relations for the Canadian Federation of Medical Students is handled by the VP Communications and the President, along with other pertinent executive members. Requests for interviews and comments are answered promptly within 24 hours or less. The CFMS is the voice of medical students in Canada and represent medical students in matters of medical education, medical student wellness and other issues of importance.

Past articles with content from the CFMS have been published at www.cmaj.ca, the Globe and Mail, Macleans and many more publications.
